WHAT YOU NEED TO KNOW ABOUT US

FFL Water Features, incorporated in August 2020, is a wholly Ghanaian owned company and an offshoot of Fin Fins Limited incorporated in February 2016. Located in Dansoman, our core business is building water features of all sorts for both corporate institutions and private residents.
‍
At FFL Water Features, we pride ourselves with providing and maintaining the highest standards of service; our client portfolio is a testament of our unique work ethic, precision and attention to detail. Having worked with world-class hospitality resorts, educational institutions, corporate organizations and heads of states, we continue to maintain the highest standards of professionalism and customer-centered services.

OUR AFFILIATIONS

We are members of the elite CAC (Certified Aquascape Contractors) group, the largest association of pond builders in the world and we are the 1st and the only Certified Aquascape Contractors in Africa having gone through rigorous training in pond building techniques and business management from the Aquascape Inc in the USA.
